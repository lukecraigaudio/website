I track, edit and mix records. I work in Nashville, primarily at Creative Caffeine Studios with producer Pino Squillace. No matter what stage of the process I'm involved in, I strive to cultivate the inchoate emotion and character that exists in songs as they bloom into beautiful records. I think an engineer's work should speak for itself, so here's mine:

Kevin Max

Starry Eyes Surprise

Tracking/Editing/Mixing
Buy it

Kevin's creativity and masterfulness as a singer make him a joy to engineer for.

  1. Under The Milky Way Tonight

    This was the first song we did for Kevin and is one of my favorites. I wanted to make the choruses sound like they were blooming up out of each downbeat, while the sax floats above and around everything.

  2. Last Night I Dreamt That Somebody Loved Me

    I grew up loving The Smiths so to do a cover with Kevin was a real treat. I even got to play my bouzouki in it. Mixing a 13 minute song is a welcome challenge because you have to find ways to keep the listener intrested. Read More I also try to keep things subtle enough so that there's a reward for multiple listenings. I recorded the cicadas in the treehouse behind Creative Caffeine and used some studio tricks to make them in rhythm with the music. I wanted the "All I want is you" background vocals to sound like giant heads looking down at the listener as spotlights illuminate them in the night. Read Less

  3. I Only Have Eyes For You

    This song has two layers of drumsets!

Aubryn

Nothin' Civil EP

Tracking/Editing/Mixing
Buy it

Aubryn is an award winning singer songwriter. She started this album with Pino around the time when I was starting to engineer at Creative Caffeine. I'm glad I was able to be the main engineer, apart from interns and assistants, to have worked on it from start to finish. It is an eclectic album, to say the least.

  1. Nothin' Civil

    This is the first song we did with Aubryn. I enjoyed bringing out the darkness in a song about domestic abuse in the mix.

  2. Cure My Disaster

    I love how the percussion gels in this one. I also played bouzouki in the bridge.

  3. In The Field

    This song was apparently inspired by the X-Files. We had Black Cat Sylvester do some surreal glitchy programming. It was intresting getting that to blend with the acoustic instruments.

  4. Lady About Town

    I also played Bouzouki and did some small programming in this one.

  5. Murder Ballad Blues

    Another song that needed to sound creepy, but this time with a blues element to it.

  6. Til The Reaper Comes

    For this bonus track, I wanted to create a sound similiar to trains without actually using any train sound.

Ben Sturgell

Music Box

Buy it

Music Box was the first project I was involved with when I started working with Pino at Creative Caffeine. I was involved in tracking and editing almost all of the songs. I am greatful to have been able to mix one of the songs.

  • Drive (Tracking/Editing/Mixing)

    While I was tracking Ben's vocals, I suggested, based on his lyrics, that he try to give a performance that sounded like a controlled anger bubling up to the surface. I reinforced this with the direction I took in the mix.

Adam Searan

Tracking/Editing/Mixing
Adam

Adam is an up and coming country music star who placed #2 in Nash Next 2015. We had been using Adam as a session player on some of other projects, so when he came to us to for his song Rescue we were more than happy to help.

  • Rescue

    I loved the creative exploration Adam allowed me and Pino to try on this song. This mix has some of the more complexly automated vocal effects I've done in a mix. I played bouzouki and xylophone in the ending jam.
